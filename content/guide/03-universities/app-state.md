### Transcript
- **Official Homeschool Transcript**: Required

### Standardized Testing
- **Test-Optional**: Yes, if weighted GPA 2.8 or above

  - Official scores required upon enrollment if admitted
- **Superscoring**: Yes - App State superscores both SAT and ACT across multiple test dates

## Homeschool-Specific Requirements

### Homeschool Policy
**IMPORTANT**: No specific homeschool admission requirements or policies were identified in public documentation. Homeschool applicants should contact App State Office of Admissions directly for guidance.

### Contact for Homeschool Questions
- **Office of Admissions**: Contact through [admissions.appstate.edu](https://admissions.appstate.edu/)
- **Address**: Appalachian State University, Boone, NC 28608
- **Recommendation**: Call or email admissions office to discuss:
  - Transcript requirements and evaluation procedures
  - Standardized testing expectations
  - Course documentation and curricula review
  - Any supplemental materials that may strengthen applications

### Transcript Format
- **Format**: Flexible - parent-created transcript accepted
- **Must Include**:
  - Student name and contact information
  - Homeschool name (if applicable)
  - Courses listed by year (9th-12th grade)
  - Grades for each course
  - GPA calculation (weighted or unweighted)
  - Grading scale used
  - Parent/administrator signature

### GPA Documentation
- **Parent-Calculated GPA**: Accepted
- **Weighted or Unweighted**: Either is fine (specify which on transcript)
- **Grading Scale**: Must document scale used (e.g., 90-100 = A, 80-89 = B)

### Course Descriptions
- **Required**: Verify with admissions office
- **Recommended**: For non-standard course titles

### Third-Party Validation
- **Required**: No
- **Recommended**: Yes - strengthens application
- **Options**:
  - SAT/ACT scores (if submitting)
  - AP Exam scores
  - Dual enrollment courses with grades
  - Community college transcripts
  - Standardized testing history

### Homeschool Supplement Form
- **Required**: Verify with admissions office
- **Common App School Report**: Parent completes as "counselor"

### NC Homeschool Documentation
- **NC Homeschool Registration**: Not required to submit
- **Annual Testing**: Not required to submit
- **Compliance**: Must comply with NC homeschool law, but proof not needed for admissions

### Letters of Recommendation
- **Requirement**: Verify with admissions office
- **For Homeschoolers**: Can be from tutors, coaches, mentors, community college professors, dual enrollment instructors, online course teachers, clergy, employers

### Homeschool-Friendliness Rating
**⚠️ Unknown - Contact Required** - App State does not publish specific homeschool policies. Recommend contacting admissions office directly for customized guidance.

### Special Notes
- Test-optional policy (GPA 2.8+) benefits homeschoolers
- NC public university familiar with NC homeschool regulations
- Contact admissions office for homeschool-specific guidance
