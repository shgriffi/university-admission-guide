## Quick Facts

- **Location**: Raleigh, North Carolina
- **Type**: Public Research University

- **Setting**: Urban campus in state capital
- **In-State Status**: NC residents eligible for in-state tuition (verify your residency status)

## Admissions Overview

- **Acceptance Rate**: 47% (Fall 2025) - Moderately selective
- **Middle 50% SAT**: 1290-1440 (average 1360)
- **Middle 50% ACT**: 25-32 (average 29)
- **Middle 50% GPA**: 4.25-4.75 weighted (average 4.49)
- **Application Options**: Early Action (non-binding), Regular Decision

**Test-Optional**: Yes, for applicants with weighted GPA 2.8 or above. 55% of Fall 2025 first-year students applied without test scores.

**Source**: [NC State Admissions Fast Facts](https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/fast-facts/) (verified 2026-02-13)

## Application Requirements

### Application Platform
- **Common Application** OR **Coalition on Scoir** (applicant's choice)
- NC State accepts either platform equally

### Application Fee
- **Fee**: $85
- **Fee Waivers**: Available for eligible students
  - Common App fee waiver accepted
  - Coalition fee waiver accepted
  - Contact admissions office for waiver eligibility

### Essays Required

**Common App Personal Statement** (Required):
- Topic: Choose 1 of 7 Common App prompts
- Word Limit: 650 words maximum

**NC State Supplemental Essays** (2 required):
1. **First Choice Major Essay**: Why did you select this major? (250 words)
2. **Community Essay**: How do you contribute to your community? (250 words)

### Recommendations
- **Counselor Recommendation**: Required (1)
  - For homeschoolers: Parent can serve as counselor
- **Teacher Recommendations**: Optional but recommended (1-2)
  - For homeschoolers: Can be from outside instructors, tutors, mentors, coaches, clergy, community college professors

### Transcript
- **Official Homeschool Transcript**: Required
- Must include: Courses by year, grades, GPA calculation, parent signature
- Can be parent-created (no third-party accreditation required)

### Standardized Testing
- **Test-Optional**: Yes, if weighted GPA 2.8 or above
- **If submitting**: Can self-report SAT/ACT scores on application
  - Official scores required upon enrollment if admitted
  - NC State will NOT accept scores listed on high school transcript
- **Superscoring**: Yes - NC State will superscore both SAT and ACT across multiple test dates
- **Required if GPA 2.5-2.79**: Must submit test scores (minimum 930 SAT or 17 ACT)

### Other Materials
- **Activities List**: Resume or activities list (optional but recommended)
- **Portfolio**: Required ONLY for College of Design programs (Industrial Design, Architecture, Art + Design)

## Homeschool-Specific Requirements

### Transcript Format
- **Format**: No specific format required - parent-created transcript accepted
- **Must Include**:
  - Student name and contact information
  - Homeschool name (if applicable)
  - Courses listed by year (9th-12th grade)
  - Grades for each course
  - GPA calculation (weighted or unweighted)
  - Grading scale used
  - Parent/administrator signature

### GPA Documentation
- **Parent-Calculated GPA**: Accepted
- **Weighted or Unweighted**: Either is fine (specify which on transcript)
- **Grading Scale**: Must document scale used (e.g., 90-100 = A, 80-89 = B)

### Course Descriptions
- **Required**: No
- **Recommended**: Only if courses have non-standard titles
- **Not Needed**: For standard courses (e.g., Algebra II, US History)

### Third-Party Validation
- **Required**: No
- **Recommended**: Yes - strengthens application
- **Options**:
  - SAT/ACT scores (if submitting)
  - AP Exam scores
  - Dual enrollment courses with grades
  - Community college transcripts
  - Standardized testing history

### Homeschool Supplement Form
- **Required**: No specific homeschool form
- **Common App School Report**: Parent completes as "counselor"

### NC Homeschool Documentation
- **NC Homeschool Registration**: Not required to submit
- **Annual Testing**: Not required to submit (but can mention in application)
- **Compliance**: Must comply with NC homeschool law, but proof not needed for admissions

### Letters of Recommendation
- **Parent Letters**: Parent serves as counselor - completes counselor recommendation
- **Outside Recommenders**: 1-2 teacher recommendations from non-parent instructors recommended
  - Can be: tutors, coaches, mentors, community college professors, dual enrollment instructors, online course teachers, clergy, employers

### Homeschool-Friendliness Rating
**✅ Very Friendly** - NC State is highly accommodating to homeschool applicants

### Special Notes
- 55% of first-year students applied without test scores - test-optional policy widely used
- No special requirements or additional hurdles for homeschoolers
- Parent can serve as counselor without issue
- Strong homeschool applicant community at NC State

**Source**: [NC State Homeschool FAQ](https://admissions.ncsu.edu/connect/frequently-asked-questions/)

## Programs Offered (Example Career Interests)

### Business
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Business Administration (Poole College of Management)
- **Concentrations**: Accounting, Economics, Entrepreneurship, Finance, Management, Marketing, Supply Chain Management
- **Special Requirements**: None - apply directly as first-year

### Industrial Design
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Industrial Design (College of Design)
- **Special Requirements**: **Portfolio Required by November 1, 2026**
  - 15-20 pieces showcasing design/creative work
  - Portfolio review determines admission to College of Design
- **Deadline**: Portfolio due November 1 for Early Action applicants

### Architecture
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Architecture (College of Design)
- **Special Requirements**: **Portfolio Required by November 1, 2026**
  - 15-20 pieces showcasing architectural/creative work
  - Portfolio review determines admission to College of Design
- **Accreditation**: NAAB-accredited program (National Architectural Accrediting Board)

### Art
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Art + Design (College of Design)
- **Concentrations**: Graphic Design, Studio Art
- **Special Requirements**: **Portfolio Required by November 1, 2026**
  - 15-20 pieces showcasing artistic work
  - Portfolio review determines admission to College of Design

### Music Production
- **Available**: ⚠️ Partial
- **Program Name**: Music (BS degree with 2 concentrations)
  - Music Technology Concentration
  - Music Education Concentration
- **Special Requirements**: Audition may be required (verify with Music Department)
- **Note**: Not a dedicated production program; music technology is one concentration

### Film Production
- **Available**: ⚠️ Limited
- **Program Name**: Film Studies (minor available, no major)
- **Note**: NC State does NOT have a production-focused film program
- **Alternative**: Communication Media major with film courses

### Aerospace Engineering
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Aerospace Engineering (Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering)
- **Strong Program**: Well-regarded engineering program with aerospace focus
- **Special Requirements**: None - apply directly as first-year
- **ABET Accredited**: Yes

## Application Notes

### Why NC State is a Good Fit
1. **In-State Tuition**: Significant cost savings as NC resident
2. **Strong Engineering**: Excellent aerospace engineering program
3. **Design Programs**: Nationally recognized College of Design (if interested in industrial design, architecture, or art)
4. **Test-Optional**: No pressure to submit test scores if GPA is strong
5. **Homeschool-Friendly**: Very accommodating to homeschool applicants
6. **Business Programs**: Poole College of Management offers strong business education
7. **Location**: Raleigh is part of Research Triangle (job/internship opportunities)

### Application Strategy
- **Apply Early Action**: Priority for scholarships and Honors Program
- **Test-Optional Decision**: If GPA is 2.8+, students can choose whether to submit SAT/ACT
- **Portfolio Programs**: If interested in design programs, portfolio deadline is November 1
