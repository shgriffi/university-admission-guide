## Homeschool-Specific Requirements

### Homeschool Policy
**✅ Friendly** - UNC Charlotte welcomes homeschool applicants with no additional requirements beyond standard materials.

### Transcript Format
- **Format**: Official high school transcript required
- **NC Homeschool Resources**: North Carolina provides transcript templates through Division of Non-Public Education portal
- **Recommended**: Use NC Division of Non-Public Education transcript template for consistency
- **Must Include**:
  - Courses listed by year (9th-12th grade)
  - Grades for each course
  - GPA calculation (weighted recommended)
  - Grading scale used
  - Parent/administrator signature

### GPA Documentation
- **Parent-Calculated GPA**: Accepted
- **Weighted or Unweighted**: Either is fine (weighted recommended)
- **Minimum**: 2.5 weighted (UNC System requirement)
- **Test-Optional Threshold**: 2.8+ weighted GPA at end of junior year

### Course Descriptions
- **Required**: No additional requirements beyond standard transcript
- **Recommended**: Provide if courses have non-standard titles

### Third-Party Validation
- **Required**: No
- **Recommended**: Yes - strengthens application
- **Options**:
  - SAT/ACT scores (if submitting)
  - AP Exam scores
  - Dual enrollment courses with grades
  - Community college transcripts

### Homeschool Supplement Form
- **Required**: No specific homeschool form
- **Common App School Report**: Parent completes as "counselor"

### NC Homeschool Documentation
- **NC Homeschool Registration**: Not required to submit
- **Annual Testing**: Not required to submit
- **Compliance**: Must comply with NC homeschool law, but proof not needed for admissions

### NC Residency Certification
- **Required**: NC residents must complete residency certification process as part of application
- **Contact**: Residency Determination Service
  - **Phone**: 1-844-319-3640
  - **Recommendation**: Contact early to ensure residency status confirmed before application deadline

### Letters of Recommendation
- **Optional**: May strengthen application
- **For Homeschoolers**: Can be from tutors, coaches, mentors, community college professors, dual enrollment instructors, online course teachers, clergy, employers

### Homeschool-Friendliness Rating
**✅ Friendly** - UNC Charlotte welcomes homeschool applicants with no additional requirements beyond standard materials.

**Rationale**:
- No additional requirements beyond standard application
- Test-optional policy (GPA 2.8+) provides flexibility
- Recognizes NC homeschool programs
- No discriminatory policies against homeschooled students
- Straightforward application process
- Availability of NC transcript templates supports accessibility

**Source**: [UNC Charlotte Application Requirements](https://admissions.charlotte.edu/apply/first-year-students/application-requirements/)

## Programs Offered (Example Career Interests)

### Business
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Business Administration (Belk College of Business)
- **Concentrations**: Accounting, Business Analytics, Economics, Entrepreneurship, Finance, International Business, Management, Marketing, Operations and Supply Chain Management, Real Estate
- **Special Requirements**: None - apply directly as first-year
- **Accreditation**: AACSB accredited

### Industrial Design
- **Available**: ⚠️ Partial
- **Program Name**: No traditional industrial design program
- **Alternative**: Mechanical Engineering with design focus, Architecture (interior design)
- **Note**: UNC Charlotte does not have dedicated industrial design

### Architecture
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Architecture (School of Architecture)
- **Strong Program**: Well-regarded architecture program
- **Special Requirements**: Portfolio may be required (verify with department)
- **Accreditation**: NAAB-accredited program
- **Focus**: Sustainable design, urban design

### Art
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Art (Department of Art & Art History)
- **Concentrations**: Studio Art, Art History, Art Education
- **Areas of Study**: Ceramics, graphic design, illustration, painting, photography, printmaking, sculpture
- **Special Requirements**: Portfolio may be required (verify with department)

### Music Production
- **Available**: ⚠️ Partial
- **Program Name**: Music (Department of Music)
- **Note**: Not dedicated music production program
- **Focus**: Music performance, music education, music composition
- **Special Requirements**: Audition required for music programs

### Film Production
- **Available**: ⚠️ Partial
- **Program Name**: Film Studies (minor available)
- **Note**: Film studies minor (no major); limited production focus
- **Alternative**: Communication Studies with media production emphasis

### Aerospace Engineering
- **Available**: ✅ Yes
- **Program Name**: Mechanical Engineering with Aerospace Concentration (William States Lee College of Engineering)
- **Strong Program**: Can specialize in aerospace within mechanical engineering
- **Special Requirements**: None - apply directly as first-year
- **ABET Accredited**: Yes
- **Note**: UNC Charlotte has growing engineering programs

4. **Test-Optional**: No pressure to submit test scores if GPA is 2.8+
5. **Charlotte Location**: Major city with internship and job opportunities
6. **Homeschool-Friendly**: No additional requirements beyond standard materials
7. **Aerospace Option**: Mechanical engineering with aerospace concentration

### Application Strategy
- **Apply Early Action**: November 1 deadline for priority consideration
- **Test-Optional Decision**: If GPA is 2.8+, students can choose whether to submit SAT/ACT
- **NC Resident Advantage**: Contact Residency Determination Service early (1-844-319-3640)
