## Application Deadlines

Appalachian State University offers multiple application pathways with different deadline schedules:

### Early Action
- **Application Deadline**: November 1
- **Materials Due**: November 15
- **Binding**: Non-binding early action program

### Priority Deadline
- **Application Deadline**: November 30
- **Materials Due**: Not specified
- **Notes**: Priority deadline for both admission and scholarship consideration

### Regular Decision
- **Application Deadline**: February 1
- **Materials Due**: February 15
- **Notes**: Standard admission pathway

## Standardized Test Scores

Appalachian State University employs a **test-optional** admission policy with weighted GPA considerations:

### Test-Optional Policy
- **Test-Optional**: Available for applicants with weighted GPA of 2.8 or above
- **Test Required**: Required if weighted GPA is below 2.8
  - Minimum ACT: 17
  - Minimum SAT: 930

### Middle 50% Score Ranges
These ranges represent the middle 50% of admitted first-year students:
- **SAT**: 1100-1280
- **ACT**: 21-27

### Average Admitted Student Scores
- **Average SAT**: 1190
- **Average ACT**: 24

### Score Policy
- University considers scores from multiple test attempts
- Weighted super-score methodology applied

## GPA Requirements

### Average GPA
- **Average GPA of Admitted Students**: 3.96 (unweighted)

### GPA Considerations
- GPA is evaluated in context of high school curriculum rigor
- Weighted and unweighted GPAs both considered
- Test-optional policy varies based on GPA threshold (2.8 weighted)
