## Test Score Requirements

**SOURCE**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/fast-facts/ and https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/ (accessed 2026-02-13)

### SAT
- **Required/Test-Optional**: Test-optional for applicants with weighted GPA 2.8 or above
- **Middle 50% Range (2024-2025 enrolled students)**:
  - **⚠️ SAT Total: 1290-1440** (average 1360)
  - **⚠️ SAT Math: 660-740** (50th percentile: 700)
  - **⚠️ Source**: Common Data Set 2024-2025; NextAdmit.com; PrepScholar.com; Niche.com - NOT OFFICIAL UNIVERSITY WEBSITE
- **Subject Tests**: Not required (SAT Subject Tests discontinued in 2021)
- **Homeschool Policy**: Same as general applicants - test-optional with weighted GPA 2.8+
- **Source**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/
- **Year of Data**: Fall 2025 class (55% of first-year students applied without submitting test scores)

### ACT
- **Required/Test-Optional**: Test-optional for applicants with weighted GPA 2.8 or above
- **Middle 50% Range (2024-2025 enrolled students)**:
  - **⚠️ ACT Composite: 25-32** (average 29)
  - **⚠️ ACT English: 25-34** (average 30)
  - **⚠️ ACT Math: 26-32** (average 28)
  - **⚠️ Source**: Common Data Set 2024-2025; NextAdmit.com; PrepScholar.com; Niche.com; College Transitions - NOT OFFICIAL UNIVERSITY WEBSITE
- **Writing Section Required**: Not required
- **Source**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/

### Test Score Policies
- **Superscore**: YES - NC State calculates your highest composite score (super-score) as the highest sub-score from each section across your submitted exam dates. This applies to both SAT and ACT. NC State encourages submitting all scores and will consider highest sub-score from each section across test dates to create a super-score.
- **Self-Reporting Allowed**: Yes - SAT or ACT scores can be self-reported on application. NC State will NOT accept standardized test scores listed on your high school transcript.
- **Test-Optional Details**: Test-optional for applicants with weighted GPA 2.8 or above; 55% of Fall 2025 first-year students applied without test scores
- **Required Testing for Lower GPA**: Students with weighted GPA 2.5-2.79 MUST submit test scores (minimum 930 SAT or 17 ACT)
- **SAT Essay/ACT Writing**: Not required
- **Source**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/test-score-consideration-in-admission-decisions/

**VERIFICATION NOTES**: NC State practices test-optional admissions. Majority of applicants now apply without test scores.

## Academic Requirements

**SOURCE**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/fast-facts/ and https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/ (accessed 2026-02-13)

### GPA
- **Average Admitted GPA**: Not published as single number
- **Middle 50% GPA Range**:
  - Weighted GPA: 4.2–4.5
  - Unweighted GPA: 3.8–4.0
- **GPA Calculation Method**: NC State accepts both weighted and unweighted GPA; uses UNC system minimum course requirements
- **Homeschool GPA**: Must be calculated and documented on homeschool transcript with grading scale provided
- **Source**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/fast-facts/
- **Year of Data**: Fall 2025 enrolled students

### Course Requirements
**NOTE**: NC State follows "minimum course requirements (MCR) established by the Board of Governors of the University of North Carolina System"

- **English**: 4 units (including grammar, composition, and literature)
- **Math**: 4 units (including Algebra I, Geometry, Algebra II, and at least one unit beyond Algebra II; or if using NC public school sequence: Math I-III and at least one unit beyond Math III)
- **Science**: 3 units (including at least one life science unit such as biology, ecology, or zoology; and one non-life science unit such as astronomy, chemistry, earth science, environmental science, physical science, or physics)
- **Social Studies/History**: 2 units (including one unit in U.S. History)
- **Foreign Language**: NOT REQUIRED as of Fall 2025. Students may instead take two additional units from English, math, social studies, science, world languages, or computer science. However, completion of two sequential world language courses is recommended.
- **Other**: 2 additional academic course units from English, mathematics, science, social studies, world languages, or computer science
- **Total MCR**: 15 course units minimum
- **Source**: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/apply/first-year/ and https://www.northcarolina.edu/apps/policy/doc.php?id=782 (UNC System Policy Manual)
- **⚠️ Secondary Source**: UNC System MCR information (2026) - verified via UNC-Chapel Hill FAQ and UNC System Policy Manual - NOT OFFICIAL NC STATE WEBSITE

**Homeschool Notes**: Homeschool transcripts must list name of each course taken in grades 9-12, including notation for advanced, honors, AP, or dual enrolled classes. Source: https://admissions.ncsu.edu/connect/frequently-asked-questions/
